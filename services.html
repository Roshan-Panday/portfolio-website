You receive the "Holy Trinity" of formats: **AI/EPS** (Source Vector), **PNG** (Transparent), and **JPG**.

Yes, we can modernize existing logos. Select the Vector plan and provide your old file.

FILE DELIVERY FORMATS

For Thumbnail and Bulk rendering, we deliver the final product in **High-Res PNG** format only. Source PSD files are not included in these packages to protect proprietary visual styling techniques.
